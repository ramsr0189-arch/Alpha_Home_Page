    Frequently Asked Questions

    Everything you need to know about Alpha Profin

    How long does loan approval take?

    For most loan products, we provide an initial assessment within 2 hours. Complete approval typically takes 24-48 hours for personal and business loans, and 3-7 days for secured loans like housing and LAP. Our AI-powered system pre-qualifies applications instantly, significantly reducing processing time.

    What is the minimum CIBIL score required?

    While requirements vary by lender, we generally work with CIBIL scores of 650+. Scores above 750 qualify for premium rates. Even if your score is lower, we have specialized programs and can guide you on improving your credit profile for better terms.

    Do you charge any upfront fees?

    No, Alpha Profin does NOT charge any upfront fees for loan applications or consultations. We only charge a nominal processing fee after your loan is approved and disbursed. All fees are transparently disclosed before you proceed. Beware of fraudsters asking for advance payments.

    Can I get a loan without collateral?

    Yes! We offer collateral-free loans including Personal Loans (up to ₹40L), Business Loans (up to ₹50Cr for established businesses), and Professional Loans (up to ₹1Cr). Approval is based on your income, credit score, and business financials rather than collateral.

    What documents are required for a business loan?

    Typically required: (1) Business proof (GST registration, Shop Act license), (2) Bank statements (12 months), (3) ITR for last 2 years, (4) KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN), (5) Business financials. For loans above ₹10L, additional documents like audited balance sheets may be needed.

    Is prepayment allowed? Are there charges?

    Yes, prepayment is allowed on most loan products. For personal loans from NBFCs, there are typically no prepayment charges. Banks may charge 2-4% on the outstanding principal. We help you choose lenders with the most flexible prepayment terms based on your needs.

    How is Alpha Profin different from direct bank applications?

    We compare offers from 15+ banks and NBFCs to get you the best rates. Our 30 years of experience means faster approvals, personalized guidance, and higher success rates. We handle documentation, follow-ups, and negotiations on your behalf - saving you time and often securing better terms than direct applications.

    Can self-employed individuals apply?

    Absolutely! We specialize in loans for self-employed professionals, business owners, and freelancers. We have dedicated products like Professional Loans for doctors, CAs, and consultants, and Business Loans for entrepreneurs. We understand self-employed income patterns and work with lenders who do too.

    What is your loan approval success rate?

    Our approval rate is 87% for pre-qualified applications. Our AI eligibility checker and experienced team ensure we only submit applications with high approval probability. Even if one lender rejects, we have alternative options across our network of 15+ financial institutions.

    Do you offer balance transfer services?

    Yes! Our Debt Consolidation service helps you transfer high-interest loans to lower rates, potentially saving lakhs in interest. We analyze your current loans, negotiate with lenders, and handle the entire transfer process. Use our Debt Consolidation Calculator above to see potential savings.

    How secure is my personal information?

    Your data security is our top priority. We use 256-bit SSL encryption, secure cloud storage, and comply with RBI data protection guidelines. Your information is only shared with lenders you authorize and is never sold to third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

    Can I track my application status?

    Yes! After submitting your application, you'll receive a unique tracking ID. You can check status through our Client Portal, via WhatsApp, or by calling our support team. We provide real-time updates at every stage - document verification, credit check, approval, and disbursal.

    What if my loan application is rejected?

    If one lender rejects, we immediately explore alternatives from our network. We provide detailed feedback on rejection reasons and guide you on improving your profile. We also offer credit counseling to help you become eligible for better terms in 3-6 months. Our goal is long-term financial partnership, not just one transaction.

    Do you provide investment and insurance services too?

    Yes! Alpha Profin is your complete financial partner. Beyond loans, we offer Mutual Funds, SIPs, Fixed Deposits, Pension Plans, and all types of insurance (Life, Health, Term, Vehicle, Travel). Our holistic approach ensures your liabilities decrease while assets increase - true wealth building.

    How do I get started?

    Simply click "Check Eligibility" in the hero section, use our AI chatbot for instant guidance, or call +91 98765 43210. You can also check your CIBIL score above for free. Our team will guide you through the entire process - from eligibility check to loan disbursal. It's that simple!
